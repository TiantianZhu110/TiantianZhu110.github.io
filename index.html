Biography

I am a fifth year Ph.D. student at the Department of Computer Science and Technology, Harbin Institute of Technology (Shenzhen), advised by Prof. Qingcai Chen. I obtained my Master's degree from Harbin Institute of Technology (Shenzhen) in 2018. My research interests lie in information extraction, biomedical NLP, and knowledge graph.
